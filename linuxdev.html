Linus Torvalds

The creator of Linux

Biography

Torvalds began learning computer programming at age 10 on his grandfather's Commodore VIC-20. In 1988, he enrolled at the University of Helsinki, where he earned a master's degree in computer science in 1996. His thesis was titled Linux: A Portable Operating System.
